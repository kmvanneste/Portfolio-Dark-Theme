About Me

My name is Kristen Michelle Van Neste and I am a software engineer.

In May 2017 I graduated with distinction from the University of Delaware and began traveling the country as a certified Associate Wildlife Biologist. I had the incredible opportunity to work in all four corners of the United States studying wildlife ranging from bats to mountain lions. My experience as a biologist introduced me to technologies such as GIS, database management, and software development. As my life changed due to COVID-19, so did my goals. I decided to take advantage of this time in quarantine to shift my focus and pursue a career as a software engineer.
